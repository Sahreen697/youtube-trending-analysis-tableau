## Academic Project

Final Data Storytelling and Visualization project completed as part of the Bachelor of Analytics & Data Management (Honours) program at Mohawk College.

## Project Overview

This project analyzes YouTube trending video data to identify patterns in audience engagement, content performance, upload timing, and category popularity. The goal was to uncover when videos are most likely to trend and which content categories maintain audience attention for the longest periods.

## Business Problem

Content creators and digital marketers often struggle to determine the best time to publish content and which content categories generate the strongest audience engagement. This project applies data visualization and storytelling techniques to analyze YouTube trending data and identify patterns that can support content strategy and audience growth.

## Dashboard Features

* Trending video performance analysis
* Audience engagement metrics
* Upload day analysis
* Category performance comparison
* Monthly viewership trends
* Interactive filters and category selection
* Data storytelling visualizations

## Key Insights

* Education videos remained trending longer than most other categories.
* Friday generated the highest overall audience engagement.
* Tuesday recorded the highest upload activity.
* July experienced the strongest viewership performance.
* Holiday periods showed lower audience engagement and viewership levels.
* Content performance varied significantly across video categories.
